About us

A seat finder application to get the nearest location to sit and take a rest in the University of Glasgow according to various user requirements.

The objective of this application is to provide users, who happen to be at the UoG campus, with the location of a place to have a seat. Since the facilities vary in each area, users can find the most suitable one by filtering by certain requirements.

Requirements can be a socket and wifi access, food service (microwave/ hot water tap), quiet study, etc. Apart from giving information about specific spots, the app can guide users to the desired places. They are welcome to rate and leave comments on each location. For example, if the facilities are under renovation or short-term closure. This would ensure users are receiving the most up-to-date information.
